Mission Statement

This website examines the 1902 Streetcar Strike of Pawtucket through a digital platform designed to educate the general public about a significant historical event within the local community. The site allows learners to visualize the past through a curated timeline that provides an annotated interpretation of the strike without requiring visitors to sift through newspaper archives. Historic photographs from newspaper collections, compiled by the Pawtucket Public Library, enhance the visual experience. An interactive map further supports learning by helping visitors visualize Pawtucket at the turn of the twentieth century while connecting locations to stories, images, and historical context from the strike.
